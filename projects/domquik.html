DOMQUIK

A personal brand project primarily focused on creating the visual identity of the pseudonym Domquik

This cover was created to visually represent a UK Garage remix of the song “Neighbors.” I wanted to keep the vibe of the original “4 Your Eyez Only” album artwork while adding a more colorful and expressive direction.

The background features a torn paper texture in warm orange tones. A handwritten-style font gives it a DIY, authentic feel, while the clean layout keeps everything readable even at smaller sizes.

The design was made in Photoshop, with attention to texture, layout, and contrast. It reflects both the energy of the track and the aesthetic of the UKG scene.
